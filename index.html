Our Research Workflow

1
Investigate & Benchmark

Test existing open-source libraries and AI models under permissive licences. Each model is stress-tested on privacy-preserving conversational datasets.

2
Develop New Libraries

Transform research insights into openly-licensed Python packages that push the state of the art while remaining reproducible and auditable.

3
Clinical Validation

Psychologists & psychiatrists validate model outputs on real-world sessions (with full consent and anonymisation), shaping future iterations.

Focus Areas

Conversational Analysis

Categorise mental-health patterns from anonymised patient–therapist conversations.

Multi-Modal Context

Fuse mini-surveys, critical events, job & relationship data to build longitudinal mental-health maps.

Predictive Modelling

Generate short-term wellbeing forecasts to aid proactive care planning.
